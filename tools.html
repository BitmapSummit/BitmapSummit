WALLETS

Bitcoin Wallets

You need a Bitcoin wallet that supports Ordinals inscriptions to hold and manage your Bitmaps.

🟠
Unisat Wallet
unisat.io
The most popular Bitcoin Ordinals wallet. Supports BRC-20, BRC-420, and Bitmaps natively. Browser extension with built-in marketplace and inscription tools. Widely used across the ecosystem.
⭕
OKX Web3 Wallet
okx.com/web3
Multi-chain wallet with strong Bitcoin Ordinals and Bitmap support. Integrated with OKX NFT Marketplace. Supports inscription management and BRC-20 tokens. Mobile and browser extension available.
✨
Magic Eden Wallet
magiceden.io
Magic Eden's native wallet provides seamless integration with their Bitcoin NFT marketplace. Optimized for Ordinals trading with Bitmap support built in.
⬡
Xverse Wallet
xverse.app
A Bitcoin wallet built for the Ordinals era. Supports Ordinals, BRC-20, and Stacks. Clean UI, mobile-first design. One of the most user-friendly options for newcomers.
🔶
Leather Wallet
leather.io
Formerly Hiro Wallet. Supports Bitcoin, Ordinals, and Stacks. Known for its developer-friendly features and clean interface. Strong choice for builders in the ecosystem.
📝
Ordinals Wallet
ordinalswallet.com
One of the first wallets designed specifically for Ordinals. Pioneered the Blocktributes system for Bitmap. Early and important infrastructure in the ecosystem's development.
EXPLORERS & VIEWERS

Bitmap Explorers

🗺️
bitmap.land
bitmap.land
The official Bitmap ecosystem portal. Explore districts, view block visualizations, read the Bitmap whitepaper, and access Atlas OS. The primary reference site for the protocol.
🖥️
Atlas OS
atlas.land
A fully on-chain operating system inscribed on Bitcoin, compatible with Bitmaps and rare sats. Built by Blockamoto himself. Atlas HEX integration coming. The most ambitious Bitmap application ever built.
🔍
Ordinals.com
ordinals.com
Casey Rodarmor's official Ordinals explorer. Look up any inscription by number or ID. Verify Bitmap inscriptions, see the satoshi they're inscribed on, and confirm ownership history.
📡
Mempool.space
mempool.space
Real-time Bitcoin block explorer. See live block data — transactions, fees, sizes — the raw information that determines each new Bitmap's characteristics as it is mined.
🔭
Unisat Inscription Explorer
unisat.io
Search and filter Bitmap inscriptions, view wallet holdings, track inscription dates, and check availability of specific block numbers before inscribing.
🎮
BRC420.io — Bitmap Browser
brc420.io
RCSV's Bitmap metaverse browser — captures 95%+ of Bitmap ecosystem traffic. Explore your District, decorate your space with BRC-420 assets, voice chat, and trade NFTs on your own land.
INSCRIPTION TOOLS

Inscribe Your Bitmap

⚡
Unisat Inscriber
unisat.io/inscribe
The most widely used inscription tool. Enter your target block number in the format {block_height}.bitmap and inscribe it to your wallet. Supports batch inscriptions and fee customization.
🔏
Ord.io
ord.io
Community inscription interface. Browse recent inscriptions, inscribe content, and explore Ordinals by category. A clean alternative to Unisat for inscription management.
🤖
OrdinalsBot
ordinalsbot.com
API-powered inscription service. Great for developers and bulk inscriptions. Supports Bitcoin and Litecoin. Well-documented API for teams building on Bitmap programmatically.
