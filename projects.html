ACTIVE PROJECTS

Currently Building ●

🔴
RCSV / Recursiverse
ACTIVE

The most important builder team in the Bitmap ecosystem. Created BRC-420, the Bitmap Browser (95%+ traffic), Bitmap Explorer, Bitmap Game, and Bitmap War. Raised seed funding from OKX and VC firm ABCDE Capital. Building Bitcoin Layer 2 scaling solution based on Bitmap.

rcsv.io · brc420.io · bitmap.tech
🖥️
Atlas OS (Blockamoto)
ACTIVE

A fully on-chain operating system inscribed on Bitcoin, compatible with Bitmap and rare sats. Built by Bitmap's creator Bitoshi Blockamoto. Modular design with Atlas HEX coming. The most ambitious inscription-based application ever built — an actual OS living on Bitcoin forever.

atlas.land · bitmap.land
🎮
Bitmap Game
ACTIVE

A social metaverse game built on Bitmap by the RCSV team. Users can visit Districts, voice chat with friends, decorate spaces with BRC-420 assets, and trade NFTs on their own land. Integrated into Merlin Chain for enhanced performance. Pioneer of Bitmap-native gaming.

bitmap.tech/game
⚔️
Bitmap War
ACTIVE

A competitive strategy game within the Bitmap metaverse. Districts battle for control of the blockchain's virtual land. Bitmap holders use their districts as bases of operation. Integrated into Merlin Chain and the broader BRC-420 asset ecosystem.

bitmap.tech
💎
Mineral (BRC-420)
ACTIVE

An RWA (Real World Asset) concept inscription with 40,000 supply, backed by real-world assets. Integrates DeFi mechanisms into the BRC-420 ecosystem. One of the most innovative cross-disciplinary projects in Bitmap — bridging physical assets with Bitcoin inscriptions.

BRC-420 Ecosystem
🥚
Bitmap420 / Egg0
ACTIVE

The first 3D generative BRC-420 collection. Egg0 holders earn points multipliers and get priority testnet access. Generating assets using decentralized solutions so digital land can yield BRC-20s, Runes, Non-Arbitrary Tokens, and BRC-420s. Listed on Magic Eden and OKX.

bitmap420.com
🔗
Merlin Chain Integration
ACTIVE

Merlin Chain — a Bitcoin Layer 2 — natively integrated Bitmap Game, Bitmap War, Mineral, and other BRC-420 DApps at launch. Supports native Bitcoin wallets (Unisat, OKX) and email/phone login via Particle Network's account abstraction. Key scaling layer for Bitmap ecosystem DApps.

merlinchain.io
📡
Community Indexers
ACTIVE

Multiple independent teams have built Bitmap indexers — software that scans the Bitcoin blockchain for valid Bitmap inscriptions. Because Bitmap is open-source, any team can run their own indexer. These form the backbone of decentralized Bitmap verification across all platforms.

Open Source — GitHub
🌐
BitSmiley (Ecosystem)
ACTIVE

A native Bitcoin stablecoin protocol integrated with the Bitmap ecosystem via Merlin Chain. Represents the DeFi financial layer emerging around Bitmap's digital land economy — enabling collateralized lending against Bitmap assets.

SUNSET PROJECTS

Wound Down ●

The Bitmap ecosystem, like all early crypto frontiers, has seen teams that couldn't sustain momentum. These projects made important contributions before winding down — their inscriptions remain on Bitcoin forever.

ProjectTypeContributionStatus
Early BRC-420 Experiments Asset Protocol First-gen composable inscriptions. Helped shape BRC-420 spec through trial and error. Proved recursive inscriptions were viable on Bitcoin. SUNSET
Various Sub-Ecosystem Games Gaming Early gaming projects built on Bitmap before Bitmap Game. Proved user interest in metaverse gaming on Bitcoin but lacked scale/funding to continue. SUNSET
Community Visualization Tools Infrastructure Third-party Bitmap district visualizers built by community members. Superseded by RCSV's more comprehensive browser (95% traffic capture). SUNSET
Spherical Universe Concept Theory / Concept Community proposal for a spherical (rather than flat) Bitmap universe visualization. Innovative concept that didn't reach implementation but influenced 3D discussions. CONCEPT ONLY
Early Parcel-Only Marketplaces Trading Attempts to create markets for individual Parcels (transaction-level land) within Districts. Superseded by broader marketplace integration into Magic Eden and OKX. SUNSET
Even "sunset" projects leave permanent marks: their inscriptions remain on Bitcoin forever. All BRC-420 assets minted by wound-down teams are still owned by their holders, still tradeable, and still valid. The blockchain remembers everything — nothing is ever truly deleted.
