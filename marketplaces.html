TRADING TIPS

How to Buy a Bitmap

// Step-by-step guide to acquiring your first Bitmap

STEP 1: Install Unisat Wallet or OKX Web3 Wallet (browser extension)
STEP 2: Fund your wallet with BTC (Bitcoin)
// You need BTC, not wrapped BTC. Real on-chain Bitcoin.
STEP 3: Go to Magic Eden → Bitcoin → Bitmap collection
STEP 4: Browse available Bitmaps — filter by price, rarity, block number
STEP 5: Click "Buy" → Confirm transaction in your wallet
STEP 6: Wait for Bitcoin confirmation (~10 minutes)
STEP 7: Your Bitmap District is now in your wallet

// Alternative: Inscribe unclaimed blocks yourself (cheaper!)
INSCRIBE: unisat.io/inscribe → type "{block_number}.bitmap"
// Check availability first — cannot claim already-inscribed blocks
